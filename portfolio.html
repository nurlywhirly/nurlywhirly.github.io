About Me

Things I do:

Full Stack Software/Web Development

I am currently a software developer at Tessitura Network, a nonprofit software company focused on supporting the needs of arts/culture/educational organizations.

I am only here thanks to the life-changing nonprofit coding bootcamp, Ada Developers Academy, a year-long (and tuition-free!) program aimed at increasing gender diversity in the tech industry.

Photography

I, like every second person and their mom who at one point had enough money to buy a dSLR camera, have dabbled in photography. Please visit my separate photography portfolio for more.

Calligraphy

Back when I worked at Reed College, I was lucky enough to be able to participate in their Calligraphy Initiative, a free weekly class we lovingly called "Scriptorium" that taught the basics of calligraphy, from Roman Capitals to Uncials to Italics and all sorts of hand cramps in between.

Some samples of my calligraphy can be seen here.
