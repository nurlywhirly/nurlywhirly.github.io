About Me

Things I do:

Software/Full-Stack Web Development

I'm currently getting my learn on at Ada Developers Academy and am interning at Dell EMC Isilon with the Strategic Testing and Advance Regressions (STAR) team.

Some recent examples of my work:

  • San Francisco Food Trucks
    • A python command line program that prints out an alphabetically ordered list of all food trucks (10 at a time) that are open at that moment.
  • Mars Rovers
    • A ruby command line program that deals with two Mars rovers executing a queue of instructions on a rectangular grid. I utilized breadth-first search to help the rovers detour around obstacles.
  • WeeBay
    • One of the biggest assignments at Ada and our first experience using agile practices in a "large" group. Three classmates and I produced an online store using Ruby on Rails.
