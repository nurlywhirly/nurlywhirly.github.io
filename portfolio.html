Calligraphy

Back when I worked at Reed College, I was lucky enough to be able to participate in their Calligraphy Initiative, a free weekly class we lovingly called "Scriptorium" that taught the basics of calligraphy, from Roman Capitals to Uncials to Italics and all sorts of hand cramps in between.

Some samples of my calligraphy can be seen here.
